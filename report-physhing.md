# Phishing Simulation TryHackMe Report

##Overview
Analysis of phishing scenarios completed in a SOC simulation environment (TryHackMe)

##Objective
Identify phishing indicators and understand attacker techniques

##Tools
Splunk 

##Key Findings
-Suspicious sender addresses
-Malicious URLs
-Social engineering tactics (urgency, impersonation)

##Indicators of Compromise
-Phishing Links 
-Suspicious email headers

##Best Practices
-Verify sender email addresses 
-Avoid clicking suspicious or unexpected links
-Report suspicious emails 
-Implement email filtering
-Conduct regular phishing awareness training 

##Conclusion
Phishing attacks rely on deception and human error. Awareness and verification are essential for prevention
